Pigeons are awesome. They are very funny and entertaining and are varied in color.

Pigeons are friendly, unlike geese. Geese are evil.

Many people underestimate pigeons. This may be because they have always been warned against letting them drive buses.

But once people learn what pigeons can do, they will be amazed.

Pigeons are great at flying and compete in both short- and long-distance races.

They also take great care of their young by building nests in safe, secluded areas.
